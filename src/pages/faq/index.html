Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our stonework and countertop installation services. If you don't see your question here, please don't hesitate to contact us directly.

General Questions

What services does The Wooden Stone LLC offer?

We specialize in commercial stonework and countertop installation, including granite, quartz, and marble countertops for multi-family properties, apartments, and commercial buildings throughout Michigan.

What areas in Michigan do you serve?

We serve all of Michigan, with primary service areas including Detroit, Ann Arbor, Grand Rapids, Lansing, Flint, Kalamazoo, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Do you work outside of Michigan?

Yes, we also serve Ohio and Indiana for select commercial projects. Contact us to discuss your specific project requirements.

How long has The Wooden Stone been in business?

We have been serving Michigan's commercial stonework needs for over 15 years, building a reputation for quality craftsmanship and reliable service.

Installation & Process

How long does countertop installation take?

Installation time varies by project size. Small projects (10-20 units) typically take 1-2 weeks, while large multi-family projects (100+ units) may take 4-8 weeks. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your operations.

What is your installation process?

Our process includes: 1) Initial consultation and measurement, 2) Material selection and templating, 3) Fabrication in our facility, 4) Professional installation by skilled craftsmen, 5) Quality inspection and final walkthrough.

Can you work around our business hours?

Absolutely. We understand commercial projects require minimal disruption. We can work during off-hours, weekends, or coordinate with your schedule to ensure smooth operations.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate with local building inspectors to ensure compliance with commercial building codes and regulations.

Materials & Options

What countertop materials do you offer?

We offer a wide range of materials including granite, quartz, marble, soapstone, and solid surface materials. Each material has unique properties and price points to suit different project requirements.

What is the cost of countertop installation?

Costs depend on material choice, project size, and complexity. Granite typically ranges from $50-80/sq ft, quartz from $60-90/sq ft, and marble from $80-120/sq ft. Contact us for a detailed quote specific to your project.

Do you offer edge profiles and finishes?

Yes, we offer various edge profiles including straight, beveled, bullnose, and ogee edges. We also provide different finishes like polished, honed, and leathered surfaces.

Can you match existing countertops?

We can often match or closely coordinate with existing countertops. Bring us a sample or photo, and we'll help you find the best match from our extensive material selection.

Commercial Projects

What types of commercial projects do you handle?

We specialize in multi-family properties, apartment complexes, hotels, restaurants, office buildings, and other commercial facilities. Our experience includes both new construction and renovation projects.

Do you provide warranty on commercial installations?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all our commercial installations. Our workmanship warranty covers installation for 5 years, and materials come with manufacturer warranties.

Can you handle large-scale projects?

Absolutely. We have the capacity and experience to handle large-scale commercial projects with hundreds of units. Our team can efficiently manage multiple crews and coordinate with other contractors.

Do you provide project management services?

Yes, we provide full project management including scheduling, coordination with other trades, quality control, and regular progress updates to keep your project on track.

Getting Started

How do I get a quote for my project?

Contact us through our website, by phone, or email. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project requirements, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote.

What information do you need for a quote?

We'll need project details including number of units, square footage, material preferences, timeline requirements, and any specific design considerations.

How quickly can you start my project?

Start dates depend on our current schedule and project complexity. We typically can begin projects within 2-4 weeks of contract signing, but we'll provide a specific timeline during consultation.

Do you provide references from past projects?

Yes, we're happy to provide references from our extensive portfolio of completed commercial projects. You can also view examples of our work in our project portfolio section.
