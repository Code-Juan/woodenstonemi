About Us

The Wooden Stone LLC is a top-rated commercial countertop fabricator and contractor specializing in custom stone fabrication, commercial countertop installation, casework, interior doors, sink fixtures, and bath accessories for commercial and multi-family projects.

Our Mission

At The Wooden Stone, our mission is to support general contractors by delivering reliable, high-quality countertop and cabinetry solutions with industry-leading efficiency and professionalism. We specialize in commercial projects, streamlining the fabrication and installation process to help our partners stay on schedule and ahead of deadlines.

We are driven by a commitment to efficiency, teamwork, innovation, and safety—both on the jobsite and in our shop. Our goal is to build trust through consistent performance, while investing in the people who make it possible.

Our Full Package Service

We offer a complete turnkey solution for commercial countertop projects:

  1. Supply of Casework & Hardware - Coordinate and manage bulk deliveries of premium cabinetry and hardware components
  2. Casework & Hardware Installation - Strategic installation mobilization in accordance with construction schedules
  3. Countertop Field Measurements - Precise field measurements utilizing advanced laser templating technology
  4. Countertop Production - Efficient fabrication processes immediately upon completion of field measurements
  5. Finish Trim Carpentry Installation - Comprehensive finish trim installation during countertop production
  6. Countertop Installation - Complete installation of custom-fabricated countertops with superior finish quality
